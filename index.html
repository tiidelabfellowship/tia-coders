Personal statement

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment information when you sign up for an account with Bookmyevent. We use this information to provide you with the services you have requested, such as processing your bookings and payments, and communicating with you about your bookings. We may also use your personal information to improve our services and to personalize your experience on our platform.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. Bookmyevent uses cookies to improve the user experience and to help us understand how our platform is being used. We use both session cookies, which expire when you close your browser, and persistent cookies, which remain on your device until they expire or you delete them.

Why do we use cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences, such as your language preference and currency
  • Improve the functionality of our platform
  • Understand how our platform is being used
  • Deliver targeted advertising

What information do we gather specifically

We may collect information about your device, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, and device type. We may also collect information about how you use our platform, such as the pages you visit, the links you click, and the time and date of your visit. We use this information to improve our services and to understand how our platform is being used.

What third party do we share your information with

We may share your personal information with third-party service providers that help us provide our services, such as payment processors, email service providers, and customer support providers. We may also share your information with third parties for marketing purposes, such as advertising partners, as long as you have given us permission to do so.
